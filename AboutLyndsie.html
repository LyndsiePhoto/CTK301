ABOUT LYNDSIE

Lyndsie Schlink is a driven, enthusiastic, award winning professional photographer with over sixteen years of professional experience in portraiture, journalism, concert, studio, social media, and marketing and communications photography. She works full time at Illinois State University (ISU) as the senior photographer, and documents concert performances for B104, a local country music radio station. She is an explorer of light, color, and emotion in her quest to create art.
Lyndsie is a second-year graduate student in Creative Technologies at ISU where she has developed her skills in gaming culture, digital painting, video, and coding for websites.
At ISU she performs artistic, creative, and skilled photographic work each day to capture and reproduce photographic images documenting the history of life on the campus and in the studio, that reflect the full range of activities, from studio headshots to a weeklong event like Homecoming.
She determines the most creative and innovative approach to capture each client’s vision, whether it be the team of six print graphic designers, web designers, or campus clients. At B104 she creatively documents the local history of headliner and up and coming country artists and musicians who perform at concerts or festivals for use on the station’s social media and website.Lyndsie has received over 20 awards for her photography from the University Photographer’s Association of America (UPAA) and the Council for Advancement and Support of Education (CASE).
